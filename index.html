About

I am an immigrant MS Computer Science graduate from Tiruppur, Tamil Nadu, India. I also work as a Graduate Assistant in the UIC ACCC. This is my last year at UIC. I love to work in software development and web development projects, as it's so much fun than mundane low-level programming and hardware stuff (No offense). I also developed a greater degree of liking towards data science and analysis after taking the CS418 (Intro. to Data Science) class in my first semester. I was able to do more computation with data than I ever imagined and it interested me to explore more in this field. I hope my next step in exploring this field is data visualization, which is why I enrolled in this class. My favorite colors are black and purple (I guess, you figured it out from the webiste).

I have 2 dogs (back at my home), their names are Stuart and Kutty. I developed an interest towards photography in my school days, which turned into my passion now, especially photographing birds. I am an amateur birder and bird photographer for 4 years now and I have observed over 150+ species of birds. Some day I dream to be a person, who travels to all parts of the world and photograph all the birds there is.

My other interests are reading, which I picked up randomly one day and not able to bore about it since. I have set a target to read 52 books this year (pretty high for a begineer), which I am half way through it. My favorite authors are Erin Morgenstern, Oscar Wilde, Haruki Murakami and George Orwell. I also like playing racing games, FIFA and a die-hard fan of the Assassin's Creed franchise.
